Who We Are

Bridging emerging technology and young minds

AverixAI is a nonprofit built on a simple belief: the students who understand artificial intelligence today will be the ones shaping it tomorrow. We give teens structured, high-quality access to the field, guided by mentors who work in it.

Every student pairs foundational AI knowledge with a hands-on capstone project — turning theory into something they've actually built and can point to.

Innovation, inclusivity, and future-readiness sit at the center of everything we design, so students leave as creators of tomorrow's technology, not just consumers of it.
