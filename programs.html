Program Overview

A complete path from fundamentals to a finished project

Students move through a structured, mentor-guided curriculum — starting with the fundamentals of Python and machine learning, and building toward the frontier: large language models, generative AI, and prompt engineering. Every concept is reinforced with hands-on work, and the program closes with a capstone project students design, build, and present themselves.

Alongside the technical track, students get weekly mentorship from working AI professionals and dedicated time on AI ethics and career readiness — so what they leave with is judgment, not just syntax.
